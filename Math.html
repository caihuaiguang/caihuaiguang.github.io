Convexity

  • If \(\forall y,f(\cdot,y)\) is convex, then \(x \mapsto \max_y f(x, y)\) is convex.

  • If \((x,y) \mapsto f(x,y)\) is convex, then \(x \mapsto \min_y f(x, y)\) is convex.
